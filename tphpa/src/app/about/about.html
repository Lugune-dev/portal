About TPHPA

Protecting Tanzania's Plant Health Heritage

Tanzania Plant Health and Pesticides Authority - Your trusted partner in safeguarding agricultural excellence, ensuring food security, and promoting sustainable farming practices across our nation

Our Purpose & Direction

Our Mission

To safeguard and enhance plant health across Tanzania, ensure responsible pesticide usage, and foster food security through sustainable agricultural practices that benefit our nation and communities.

Our Vision

A Tanzania where plant health is optimally protected, pesticides are safely used, and agriculture contributes significantly to national development and prosperity.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our operations and interactions.

Collaboration

We actively partner with stakeholders, farmers, and communities to achieve shared goals for agricultural excellence.

Innovation

We embrace cutting-edge technologies and innovative approaches to continuously improve plant health protection.

Sustainability

We champion environmentally responsible practices that ensure long-term agricultural sustainability for future generations.

What We Do

Plant Quarantine

Preventing the introduction and spread of harmful plant pests and diseases that threaten our agricultural sector.

Pesticide Regulation

Ensuring safe, effective, and responsible use of pesticides to protect both crops and the environment.

Quality Assurance

Maintaining rigorous standards for plant health and agricultural products to guarantee quality and safety.
